Аналитик is a programming language, developed in 1968 (in USSR) at the Institute of Cybernetics of the Academy of Sciences of the Ukrainian SSR. It is the development of the АЛМИР-65 language, keeping compatibility with it.
A distinctive feature of the language are abstract data types, calculations in arbitrary algebras, and analytic transformations.
It was implemented on МИР-2 machines.
Later a version of Аналитик-74 was developed, implemented on МИР-3 machines.
At the moment the language АНАЛИТИК exists as a computer algebra system Аналитик-2010, which is being developed jointly by the Institute of Mathematical Machines and Systems of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine and the Poltava National Technical University.
